our history

the ghana shippers council was established in 1974 by NRCD254 to protect the interests of shippers in ghana and the west African sub-region.

our vision

to be the leading advocate for shippers in GHANA and the West African sub-region.

our mission

to create efficcient,competitive and sustainable shipping industry that facilitates international trade and contributes to national development.

our mandate

advocacy

representing shippers interest in policy formulation and implementation

trade facilitation

streamlining shipping and trade procedures to reduce costs and delays

information services

providing shipping market intelligence and advisory services

capacitybuilding

enhancing shippers knowledge and skills in shipping matters
