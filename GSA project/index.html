about ghana shippers council

the ghana shippers council (GSC)is a government agency established to protect the interest of shippers and provide solutions to challenges in the shipping industry.

our mission is to create an efficient, competitive and sustainable shippping industry that facilitates international trade and cintributesto national development.

our mandate includes;

  • advocacy for shippers
  • trade facilitation
  • shipping information dissemination
  • complaint resolution
  • capacity building
  • policy development
  • industry reseaarch
